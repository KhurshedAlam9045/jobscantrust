Job Descritpions

IN THIS SECTION you will find brief details about a selection of jobs which are available – that you can apply to for. We have tried to cover as many job types as possible – but we know that there will be a few that we have missed.

We hope that the following details help you when deciding which career path to take – whether it be promotional prospects in an existing career environment or changing directions – these brief details are here to assist. You know what they say – "variety is the spice of having a good life".

Administration Jobs

Is it true that you are an organised person in your personal and professional life? Is it true that you are one detail oriented person to the extent that loves extracting details even out of daily life routine and loving it . Would like to make this habit of yours a great career. Well then your career as administrator awaits you. Read More

Aerospace Jobs

This industry manufactures and assembles aircrafts, spacecraft and missiles. Aerospace companies hire different levels of education and skills – from scientists – to assemblers – to mechanics. Read More

Apprenticeships Jobs

This role combines learning, training, and real work experience. Apprentices work towards the full apprenticeship by receiving a wage or an allowance from the government. These courses are offered for more than 180 career choices including accountancy, veterinary, construction, business administration, among many other areas. Read More

Architechs & Construction Jobs

Entering the construction industry isn’t a get-rich-quick scheme. However, starting salaries are typically in line with averages in other graduate career areas, and will be a nice little earner a few years down the line. Read More

Arts & Crafts Jobs

Whether you’re designing ships as a naval architect or creating cutting edge art as a fine artist, the one thing that all jobs in this family have in common is creativity. So if you’re an ideas person and you like creating things that are useful and visually appealing.  Read More

Banking & Finance Jobs

This career provides financial services to clients at the top end of the market, such as corporations, institutions and governments. Amongst other services, clients are assisted to raise loans, invest surplus cash, take over and merge with other businesses or float shares on the stock exchange. Read More

Brand Manager Jobs

First impression is the last. If you are some who believe in these words. Then you are sure to get success in the field of marketing. It takes a person like you to manage the appearance and integrity of the brand you work for. Read More
